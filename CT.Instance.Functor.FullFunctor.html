Full Functors

A "full" functor is one that is surjective on hom-sets. That is, A functor \(F : C \to D\) is full if \(\forall x, y \in C\), the function \(F : C(x, y) \to D(F(x), F(y))\) is surjective.
So we require a Functor and a proof that surjectivity of hom-sets is satisfied.
